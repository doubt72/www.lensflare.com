Contact Us

If you have any issues with Election Manager 2016 or want to contact us about it for any reason, please feel free to send us a note at support@lensflare.com. We're happy to hear from you, no matter if you've found a bug (we'd really like to fix those!), want to tell us what you think of the game (good or bad), or just have questions.

You can also reach us at @lensflare_games on twitter. Alternately, visit our page on Facebook.
