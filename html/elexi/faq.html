Elexi Frequently Asked Questions

Here be questions and answers about Elexi — Feel free to contact us if you have other questions not covered here.

Supported Platforms

What platforms are supported?

Most Android and iOS devices, including both phones and tablets (as well as other compatible devices, e.g., iPod Touch, but not including Android TV devices or watches and the like).

Can I play the iPad version on my iPhone and vice-versa?

Elexi will be universal, so will be able to be played on either type of device with one purchase.

Will Elexi be available on any other platforms?

No, there are no plans for any other platforms at this time.

Other Questions

Why can't I play a one-letter word to clear the board?

All words have to be at least two letters long in Elexi, so you'll always end a game with at least one tile left.
